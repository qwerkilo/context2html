第三章：技术趋势与展望

Chapter 3: Technology Trends & Outlook

传统冯·诺依曼架构有一个死穴：存储墙。数据在处理器和内存之间来回搬运，功耗和延迟就在这里烧掉了。存算一体（CIM）的解法很直接——在存储单元里直接算。对大规模神经网络推理来说，这个思路特别对路。

Computing-in-Memory (CIM) architecture is a key technology direction for AI chips. The traditional von Neumann architecture suffers from the memory wall bottleneck, where data movement between processor and memory consumes significant power and latency. CIM overcomes this by performing computation directly within memory cells, dramatically reducing data movement overhead, especially for large-scale neural network inference.

更大的芯片需要更好的封装。NVIDIA、AMD 的高端 AI 加速器已经全线转向多芯片模块（MCM）。这导致台积电的 CoWoS 产能被抢购一空——它反过来成了制约 AI 芯片出货的瓶颈。先进封装，现在谁抢到产能谁就有货卖。

Advanced packaging, especially CoWoS (Chip-on-Wafer-on-Substrate) and 3D packaging, is becoming a critical bottleneck for next-generation AI chips. As chip sizes grow, high-end AI accelerators from NVIDIA, AMD and others have adopted multi-chip module (MCM) designs. TSMC's CoWoS capacity is in short supply, becoming a key constraint on AI chip shipments.

再往远处看，光互连和 Chiplet 是绕不开的方向。前者用光信号取代电信号，芯片间带宽更高、功耗更低；后者让厂商混搭不同工艺节点造模块。我们估计，到 2028 年六成以上的高端 AI 芯片会走向 Chiplet。

Silicon photonics and chiplet architectures are important medium-to-long-term trends. Silicon photonics enables higher bandwidth and lower power data transmission between chips, while chiplet designs allow mixing different process nodes to reduce cost and design complexity. By 2028, over 60% of high-end AI chips are expected to adopt chiplet architectures.
